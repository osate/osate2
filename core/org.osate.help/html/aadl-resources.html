AADL Resources

AADL on the Web

For people that would like to learn more about AADL and contribute to the community, the following online resources are useful:

  • The SAE standard page
  • The SAEAADL Github development area

Software Engineering Institute

The Software Engineering Institute is one of the core contributors to the AADL.

  • SEI has multiple materials around AADL gathered as an online collection: SEI AADL collection. It features webinars, blog posts, podcasts, technical reports and publications.
  • SEI AADL page, has additional information on SEI contributions to AADL and links to trainings.

OpenAADL.org

J. Hugues maintains a website with some of its contributions on AADL OpenAADL.org. This includes examples and tutorials.

Mailing lists

Three mailing lists exist to discuss AADL topics:

  • sae-aadl to discuss AADL standardization topics.
  • sae-aadl-users to discuss AADL usage.
  • OSATE Google group to discuss OSATE-related topics.
